HUBBUB

The final 6 months programme from dai hall.

In HUBBUB, ame will introduce three residencies and music/sound art programmes during 2023: Digital Gardens, Zero Parameters, and Winter Process.

HUBBUB will represent a diversity of performance art & culture, commissioning marginal and experimental artists and musicians from all over the world to deliver inspiring works as a form of residency, music performance, exhibition and workshop at Dai Hall situated at the heart of Kirklees.
