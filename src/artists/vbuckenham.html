v buckenham is a artist who works with technology & play. They like to explore generative systems, and they like to make tools that let other people explore generative systems. They are currently at work on Downpour, a creation tool for hypertext collages. Previously, they have worked on the award-winning video games Beasts of Balance, Mutazione and Panoramical, as well as co-founding the festival of play Now Play This.
