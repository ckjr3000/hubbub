Bioni Samp is an artist, music producer, and beekeeper. He started performing 20 years ago with a series of beekeeping performances titled 'Beespace or Hive Synthesis'. Hive Synthesis attempts to create a symbiotic frequency relationship, like pollination and nectar exchanges, beekeeper and bees.

He has performed across Europe, Scandinavia, and Canadia at arts and ecology festivals.
