Alex McLean is a Sheffield-based algorithmic musician and live coder.

He co-founded the Algorave movement, Algomech festival, and created the free/open source TidalCycles live coding software. Alex has performed live coded music widely including at Sonar, No Bounds, Glastonbury, Transmediale and Ars Electronica festivals. By day he researches new creative tools for making algorithmic patterns, inspired by ancient technologies, as part of the non-profit organisation Then Try This.
