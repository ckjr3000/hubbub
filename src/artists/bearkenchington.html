"For many years I have been working in the field of human powered, mechanically assisted acoustic instruments. I am interested in how, in this relationship, the humans are mechanised and the machines are humanised. I never use electricity or any form of digital technologies in my instruments, although I often explore similar sound worlds, loops, drones, non-specific pitch and song noise. I would describe it as post digital. I imagine my machines in a future world making music for renegade humans hiding in caves from the robots."
