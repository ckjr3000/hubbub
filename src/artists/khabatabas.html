Khabat Abas is an experimental, improvisational cellist, and composer from Kurdistan-Iraq. She considers herself a rule-breaker within the world of cellos by experimenting with cello both structurally and musically, she is pushing the instrument's boundaries beyond recognition. She creates diverse cellos and uses her own body to innovate and develop a personal sound language. She continues to push the boundaries of the instrument and develop her innovative language.

Khabat has performed in solo and group performances in the Middle East, Europe, Australia, and the USA.
