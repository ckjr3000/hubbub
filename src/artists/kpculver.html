KP Culver is an artist who plays with spatial boundaries through performance, writing, sound and installations. Identifying direct actions that focus on how environments choreograph bodies becomes an everyday practice and intervention of navigating spaces. By doing so the works explore social and political histories through the construct of site. These threads of investigation invite the audience to consider a deeper reflection of our vulnerabilities, politics and aspirations.
