Friday 1st September

Workshops with I. Nakhla, V Buckenham, Bear Kenchington

All workshops are pay as you feel. We can take card or cash payments on the day.

  • 15.00 - 16.30: I. Nakhla

    Nakhla’s workshop will explore how audio sculptures can be assembled out of accessible, sonic archives (on phones and computers) – focusing on rhythm, improvisation, and play. Nakhla is interested in this practice as a form of ‘counter-archiving’; a way of re-appropriating, through re- using, sound and its technologies.

  • 16.30 - 18.00: V Buckenham

    Join v buckenham for an exploration of their new tool, which puts together letterforms into strange and pleasing combinations and patterns.
